I've been writing computer music since 2009, and currently produce in FL Studio 12 (and sometimes Ableton Live). I compose in a variety of genres and styles, but I'm most consistent in writing chiptune-fusion styled tunes, and scratch the surface of some jazz and electro.









Since I've started producing, I've gotten to know some wonderful people who run really great music groups, such as my affiliate DESKPOP Music. I've been working with them for almost two years now, and our content quality and scale for our musical niche has grown exponentially. I've also worked with some smaller labels, like Boyphonics. They're both wonderful groups run by some really talented and outgoing people; working with them has been a pleasure.
