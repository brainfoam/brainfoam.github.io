I'm an artist, musician, designer, and programmer from Long Island, NY. I sometimes go by the name "brainfoam," which has been my musical and artistic alias for a little over three years now. I have pursued a bachelors degree in Game Design and Development from the Rochester Institute of Technology.

Creating art has always been a huge staple in my life, getting me through all my highs and lows. I've been drawing since I was able to pick up a pencil, and have started doing 3D renditions of my characters and concepts in the more recent years. Going to school for games has taught me how to express my ideas and thoughts through an interactive medium, which I find to be incredibly meaningful. Thanks for reading this little blurb about me!
