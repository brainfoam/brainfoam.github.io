My art consists mostly of mixed-media pieces, created in Autodesk 3DS Max, Autodesk Maya, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. I also illustrate by-hand, but I don't like to showcase what I've done since many pieces are unfinished or not in a presentable state.

Some of my pieces have been popularized and used under various music record labels and artistic individuals, such as my affiliates DESKPOP Music, HYPERPOP Collective, Nelward, Shady Monk, and many, many more.
